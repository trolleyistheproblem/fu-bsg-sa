INSTRUCTIONS: In this study, you will be in a group of 5 players chosen at random, and you will play 12 rounds of this game. One or more of the players will be automated. You will be given an endowment of {{ Constants.endowment }} each round to use to make a joint investment with the group. Each player decides how much he/she puts into the investment. This will be your round input. Each round, your input can be any amount from ₹0 to {{ Constants.endowment }}. Your round total will be calculated as follows:

  • In each round, the inputs of all 5 members are added up.
  • The total is then is multiplied by {{ Constants.efficiency_factor }}.
  • After being multiplied by {{ Constants.efficiency_factor }}, the new total is evenly split among the 5 members in that round. This will be your individual share.
At the end of the rounds, you will get to keep your individual share, plus the amount you did not put in at the start of each round. There will be 12 rounds and the entire process will take 30 to 40 minutes. You can potentially earn up to ₹360 at the end of the game.
